**SLDS Icon-only Button** — a compact action represented by a single icon; always pass `title` for the accessible name + tooltip.

```jsx
<ButtonIcon iconName="utility:settings" variant="bare" title="Setup" />
<ButtonIcon iconName="utility:edit" variant="border" title="Edit" />
```

Variants: `border` · `border-filled` · `bare` (toolbars/headers) · `brand` · `container`. Sizes: `x-small`–`large`.
