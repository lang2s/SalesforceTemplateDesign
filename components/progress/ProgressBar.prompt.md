**SLDS Progress Bar** — a horizontal completion indicator (0–100).

```jsx
<ProgressBar value={68} label="Quota attainment" showValue />
<ProgressBar value={100} variant="success" size="small" />
```

Props: `value`, `size` (`small`·`medium`·`large`), `variant` (`base`·`success`·`warning`), `showValue`, `label`.
